About Me

I'm a backend-focused product developer currently pursuing my B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering at IIT Delhi, where I represent the Mechanical Department. As a U.S. citizen with expertise in Java, Spring Boot, REST APIs, and Generative AI, I build modular backend architectures that seamlessly integrate with LLMs and modern AI pipelines.

Currently working at Naviga Global as a Generative AI intern, I'm implementing microservices in Java 21, building REST endpoints, and integrating local LLMs using Ollama for scalable content processing. My main project involves implementing the MCP protocol to convert SharePoint-hosted PDFs, Word Docs, and PPTs into vector-ready chunks for LLM-powered conversational agents, optimized for corpora exceeding 1000 pages with response latencies under 300ms.

My other projects showcase real-world AI applications, including a News Article Bias Detector that uses LLMs to analyze raw HTML content and flag biased media, reducing review time from 10 minutes to under a second, and a Summarizer that creates concise, context-aware summaries of web articles with sub-200ms API response times.
